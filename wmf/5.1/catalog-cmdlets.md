## <a name="test-filecatalog"></a><span data-ttu-id="8c698-118">Test-FileCatalog</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8c698-118">Test-FileCatalog</span></span>

![](../images/TestFileCatalog.jpg)
